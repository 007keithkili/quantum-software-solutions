Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What is your typical response time?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 2 business hours during office hours. For urgent matters, WhatsApp messages are answered fastest. For existing clients, we offer 24/7 emergency support.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a free 30-minute consultation for all new projects. This can be done via phone, video call, or in-person at our office. During this consultation, we'll discuss your project requirements and provide initial recommendations.

What information should I provide when contacting you?

To help us serve you better, please include: 1) Your name and contact information, 2) Company name (if applicable), 3) Type of project or service needed, 4) Project budget range, 5) Desired timeline, and 6) Any specific requirements or preferences.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While we prefer appointments to ensure we can give you our full attention, walk-ins are welcome during office hours. If a team member is available, we'll be happy to meet with you. For guaranteed availability, we recommend scheduling an appointment in advance.

What are your payment terms?

For most projects, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards.
