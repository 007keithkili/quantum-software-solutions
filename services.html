Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Most of our packages include 1-6 months of free maintenance depending on the plan.

Can you work with existing brands or only create new ones?

We work with both! Whether you need a complete rebrand or want to refresh your existing identity, we can help. We'll work with your current brand elements while enhancing and modernizing your visual identity.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. For projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

We can help you purchase and set up hosting and domains. We partner with reliable hosting providers and can manage everything for you as part of our service package.
